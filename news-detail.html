Free Health Checkup Camp Success

DH Hospital successfully conducted a free health checkup camp last weekend, benefiting over 500 local residents. Our team of specialists provided consultancies in Cardiology, Orthopedics, and General Medicine.

At DH Hospital, we believe that quality healthcare should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial background.

Following the inauguration of our new diagnostic wing, we are proud to offer advanced screening services at subsidized rates during these camps. We thank all our medical staff and volunteers for their dedication.

  • Free Blood Pressure and Sugar monitoring
  • Specialist consultations for elderly patients
  • Distribution of essential health supplements

We plan to host such camps monthly. Stay tuned to our news section for the next camp date and location.
